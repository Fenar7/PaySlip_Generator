# Payment Issues & Dunning

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails, your subscription enters a "Past Due" state. Slipwise will:
1. Retry the payment automatically (up to 3 attempts over 7 days)
2. Send email notifications at each retry
3. If all retries fail, the subscription may be suspended

## Resolving Payment Issues

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Update your payment method
3. Click "Retry Payment" to trigger an immediate charge

## Dunning Notifications

Slipwise uses intelligent dunning based on customer behavior:
- **Gentle Reminder** — Sent on the day payment is due
- **Firm Follow-up** — Sent 3 days after failure
- **Final Notice** — Sent 7 days after failure with suspension warning

## Disputes & Refunds

For billing disputes or refund requests, contact support@slipwise.com with:
- Your organization name
- The invoice number in question
- A description of the issue

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
