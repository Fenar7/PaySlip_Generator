# Subscription Plans & Billing

## Available Plans

| Plan | Invoices | Team | Key Features |
|------|----------|------|--------------|
| **Free** | 10/month | 2 users | Basic invoicing, 1 template |
| **Pro** | Unlimited | 10 users | All suites, analytics, dunning |
| **Enterprise** | Unlimited | Unlimited | SSO, custom roles, API, priority support |

## Managing Your Subscription

Navigate to **Settings → Billing** to:
- View your current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade plans
- Update payment methods
- View billing history and download invoices

## Payment Methods

- **International** — Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX)
- **India** — Razorpay (UPI, Net Banking, Cards)

Your payment gateway is automatically selected based on your billing country and currency.

## Metered Billing

Some features are usage-based and billed on top of your subscription:
- **Pixel Jobs** — OCR processing and image manipulation
- **PDF Studio** — Custom PDF generation beyond plan limits

Usage is tracked in real-time and billed at the end of each cycle.

## Invoices

A Slipwise-branded subscription invoice is automatically generated for every successful payment. Download past invoices from Settings → Billing → History.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period. After that, it downgrades to the Free plan (data is preserved).
